Baked Squash
    Butternut Squash, each one halved lengthwise and seeded
  • Place squash, cut sides down, on an aluminum foil-covered cookie sheet
  • Bake in oven at 375 degrees for 40 minutes. Squash should be tender and easily pierced with a fork.
  • Let cool, and scoop out flesh.
    Used in: Senator Russell Potatoes, Butternut Squash Risotto, Cranberry Squash Pasta Salad, and Squash Pie

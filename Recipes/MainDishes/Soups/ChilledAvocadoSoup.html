Chilled Avocado Soup
    1 medium ripe Haas avocado
    2 cups buttermilk
    2 tablespoons lemon juice
    1 medium tomato, seeded and diced
    1 small scallion, thinly sliced
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    cayenne pepper to taste
  • Cut avocado and remove flesh.
  • Combine avocado, buttermilk, lemon juice, salt, and cayenne pepper in food processor or blender until smooth. Pour into medium bowl.
  • Set aside small amount (1 tablespoon?) of tomato and scallion, and stir the remaining tomato and scallion into soup.
  • Cover and chill for 1 hour. Top with tomato and scallion.

Pesto
    1 bunch pesto (enough to be a little below the blade of the food processor)
    3-4 cloves garlic, lightly minced
    1/4 cup pine nuts
    3/4 cup olive oil
    3/4 cup parmesan cheese
    Pasta (I use rotini, but bowtie and others work, too)
  • Put all ingredients except pasta into a food processor. Run food processor until sauce is creamy. Serve over pasta.
  • Note that this recipe creates about 12 ounces of sauce. For a pound of pasta, use 8 ounces of sauce. Alternatively, you can use 1 1/2 pounds of pasta for 12 ounces of sauce. But you're good at math, so I didn't really need to tell you that.

Graham Pie Crust
    10-11 full sheet graham crackers
    5-6 Tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    1/4-1/3 cup sugar
  • Use a food processor or blender to grind up graham crackers. Or crush them in a zipped top bag with a rolling pin.
  • Mix the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar together with a rubber spatula in a medium bowl until combined. The mixture will be thick, coarse, and sandy. Press the mixture into the bottom of the greased dish or pan and slightly up the sides. Make sure it is tight and compact. Use the bottom of a flat measuring cup. The crust will be thick.
  • For a baked dessert, pre-bake for 10 minutes at 300°F or 7-8 minutes at 350°F. For a no-bake dessert, chill the crust for 2 hours before using.
    Used in: Chocolate Silk Pie

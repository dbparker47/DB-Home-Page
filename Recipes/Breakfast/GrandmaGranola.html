Grandma's Granola (Joy)
    4 cups oats
    1 1/2 cups sliced almonds
    1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
    1/4 cup canola oil
    1/4 cup honey
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1 cup dried cranberries
    1/2 cup dried blueberries
  • In a large bowl, mix oats, almonds, brown sugar, salt, and cinnamon.
  • In a small pan, warm the canola oil and honey. Whisk in vanilla.
  • Carefully pour over liquid over oat mixture, and Stir gently with a wooden spoon.
  • Spread granola in a 15'' x 10'' x 1'' pan, and bake at 300 degrees for 40 minutes. Stir carefully every 10 minutes.
  • Transfer granola-filled pan to a wire rack and cool completely. Stir in dried fruit.
  • Seal granola in an airtight container or self-sealing plastic bag. Store at room temperature for a week or in the freezer for up to 3 months.

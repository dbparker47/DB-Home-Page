Famous Barr French Onion Soup
    3 lbs yellow onions, peeled and thinly sliced
    1/2 cups butter
    2 T paprika
    1 bay leaf
    3/4 cup flour
    3 quarts vegetable broth
    2 t salt
    1 cup dry wine
  • Melt the butter in a Dutch oven. Add the slices onions and saute slowly over low heat for about 1 1/2 to 2 hours.
  • Add the paprika, bay leaf, and flour. Stir to mix. Saute over low heat for another 10 minutes.
  • Slowly add the broth (it is easier to avoid lumps if the broth is hot). Then add the wine.
  • Simmer uncovered for about 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Season to taste with salt.
  • Serve soup in bowls, top with a slice of french bread and top with gruyere (or swiss and mozzarella). Melt the cheese.

Brownies
    1/2 cup butter
    1 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    1/3 cup cocoa powder
    1/2 cup flour
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    Chocolate chips (optional)
    1/4 teaspoon baking powder (optional)
  • Grease and flour an 8x8 inch pan
  • Melt butter in a large saucepan. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Beat in cocoa powder, flour, salt and baking powder. Spread into prepared pan.
  • Bake, preheated, at 350 degrees for 29 minutes without chocolate chips and 31 minutes with chocolate chips. Do not overcook. Yield: 16 brownies.

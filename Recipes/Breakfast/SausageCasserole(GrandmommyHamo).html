Sausage Casserole (Grandmommy Hamo)
    5 slices firm bread, cubed
    1 pound sausage
    1 1/2 cup grated cheese
    9 eggs, beaten
    2 cups milk or half-and-half
    1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • Place half of the bread into a greased 9'' x 13'' baking dish.
  • Sprinkle in cheese, sausage, and the rest of the bread.
  • Pour the mixture of the eggs, milk, salt, and mustard into the baking dish.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

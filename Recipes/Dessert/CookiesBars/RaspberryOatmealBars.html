Raspberry Oatmeal Bars
    1 cup all-purpose flour
    1 cup rolled oats
    ½ cup packed light brown sugar
    ¼ teaspoon baking soda
    ⅛ teaspoon salt
    ½ cup butter, softened
    ¾ cup seedless raspberry jam
  • Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Rub in butter using a silicone spatula to form a crumbly mixture.
  • Line 8" x 8" square pan with greased foil. Press 2 cups oat mixture into the bottom of the pan.
  • Spread jam to within 1/4 inch of the edge.
  • Sprinkle remaining crumb mixture on top; press down lightly into jam.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Let it cool before cutting into bars.

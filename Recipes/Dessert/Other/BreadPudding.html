Bread Pudding
    4 packed cups of cubed bread
    3 eggs
    3 cups milk
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    2 teaspoons vanilla
    1 cup white chocolate chips
  • Spread bread in 9'' x 13'' baking pan.
  • Beat together remaining ingredients. Pour over bread.
  • Bake at 350 degrees until firm but not dry (35 minutes).

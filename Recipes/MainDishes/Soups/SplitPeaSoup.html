Split Pea Soup
    1/2 lb. sausage or ham
    1 lb. split peas
    6+ cups water
    5-6 carrots
    1 large onion
    1 teaspoon dried parsley
    3+ ribs celery
    2 bay leaves
    salt and pepper to taste
  • Cook carrots, celery, and onion patiently in a pot.
  • Toss everything else into the pot.
  • Bring to a boil.
  • Simmer for 1.5 hours

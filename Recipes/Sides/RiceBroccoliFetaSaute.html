Rice, Broccoli, and Feta Cheese Saute
    1 cup rice
    2 cups water
    1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
    2 teaspoons canola oil
    1/4 cup olive oil
    4 cloves garlic, minced
    2 medium tomatoes, diced
    1 bunch broccoli, stalks peeled and cut small (5 cups)
    1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    1/4 cup water
    5 ounces crumbled feta cheese
    black pepper (optional)
  • Combine rice, water, salt, and oil in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Lower heat to simmer, and cook until all water is absorbed. Remove from heat and keep covered.
  • In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Saute garlic for 2 minutes. Add tomatoes and saute for 2 minutes. Add broccoli and oregano, toss well, and add remaining water. Cover and raise heat to medium high. Cook for 5 minutes.
  • Stir in hot rice, feta cheese, and black pepper to taste.

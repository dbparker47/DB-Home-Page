Doughnuts - Apple Cider
    Doughnuts
    1/2 cup apple cider
    1 3/4 cups flour
    1/2 cup sugar
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    Pinch of salt
    1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
    Pinch of nutmeg
    1/4 cup buttermilk
    2 tablespoons butter, melted
    1 egg, lightly beaten
    Glaze
    1/3 cup confectioner's sugar
    1 tablespoon cider
  • Place cider in a small saucepan on stove set to medium-high and reduce to 2 tablespoons; cool completely.
  • Combine all dry ingredients, except sugar, in one mixing bowl; combine all wet ingredients, plus sugar, in another mixing bowl.
  • Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients; mix until just fully incorporated.
  • Cook in doughnut maker.
  • Whisk together confectioner's sugar and cider; brush doughnut tops with glaze.

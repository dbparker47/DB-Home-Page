Popcorn Toppings
    Sweet and Salty (For 1/4 cup popcorn)
    1 tablespoon butter
    1 tablespoon honey
    1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    Salt to taste
  • Melt butter; add other ingredients; and then combine, keeping warm while popcorn pops.
    Cinnamon and Sugar (For 1/4 cup popcorn)
    3 tablespoons sugar
    1 tablespoon butter
    1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
    Salt to taste
  • Melt butter; add other ingredients; and then combine, keeping warm while popcorn pops.
  • Recipe says to bake at 250 degrees for 10 minutes, and let sit in turned off oven for 20 minutes.

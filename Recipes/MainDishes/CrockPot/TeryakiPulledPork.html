Teryaki Pulled Pork
    3-4 pounds boneless pork shoulder
    1 cup brown sugar, packed
    1/3 cup unsweetened apple juice
    1/3 cup soy sauce
    1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1/4 teaspoon pepper (optional)
    2 tablespoons cornstarch
    3 tablespoons cold water
  • Cut roast in half and rub with brown sugar. Place in crockpot. Pour apple juice and soy sauce over the roast.
  • Cover and cook on low for 6-6 1/2 hours or until meat is tender.
  • Remove roast; cover and let stand for 15 minutes, straining juices. Return everything to the crockpot.
  • Combine cornstarch and cold water until smooth, and gradually stir it into the juices.
  • Cover and cook on high for 15 minutes, or until thickened.

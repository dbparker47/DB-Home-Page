Sweet Potato Pie
    Half-recipe of Regular Pie Crust
    3 medium sweet potatoes, baked (3 cups, cooked)
    1/2 cup butter, melted
    1 cup brown sugar
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    2 large eggs, beaten
    1 cup milk, whole or evaporated
    1 teaspoon cinnamon (less?)
    1 teaspoon ground or grated fresh ginger (less?)
    1/2 teaspoon nutmeg (less?)
    1/8 teaspoon ground cloves or allspice (less?)
  • Mix together filling ingredients
  • Pour filling into crust.
  • Bake at 400 degrees for 60 minutes, or until center of pie barely jiggles when you shake it.

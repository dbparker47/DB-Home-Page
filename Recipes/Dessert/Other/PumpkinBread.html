Pumpkin Bread
    3 cups sugar
    3 1/2 cups flour
    1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    1 1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 teaspoon cloves
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
    1 teaspoon nutmeg
    4 eggs
    1 cup cooking oil
    2/3 cup water
    2 cups pumkin puree
  • Place all dry ingredients (through nutmeg) in a bowl, and make a hole in the middle. Mix in wet ingredients.
  • Put mixture in greased and floured pans. Bake 1 to 1 1/2 hours at 325 degrees. Makes 4 small loaves.

Upside Down Peach Crisp (Mug)
    2 teaspoons oil
    2 tablespoons oats (asks for quick oats, soak old-fashioned oats?)
    1 tablespoon chopped pecans
    1 teaspoon flour
    1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
    1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    1 peach, chopped
  • Briefly microwave oil in a 16-oz mug to thin it out. Stir in oats, pecans, brown sugar, flour, lemon zest, and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon.
  • In a bowl, toss peach, flour, and 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon. Add peaches to mug and microwave until bubbly, about 2 minutes.

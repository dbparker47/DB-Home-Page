Chocolate Chip Cookies
    1 cup (two sticks) butter
    1 cup sugar
    1 cup packed brown sugar
    2 eggs
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    1 teaspoon baking soda, dissolved in 2 teaspoons hot water
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    3 cups flour
    2 cups chocolate chips
    1 cup chopped walnuts
  • Cream together butter, sugar, brown sugar until smooth
  • Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then stir in vanilla. Add in dissolved baking soda and salt.
  • Stir in flour, chololate chips, and nuts. Drop large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans (20 cookies per sheet).
  • Bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees, or until edges are nicely browned.
  • For air fryer, bake 4 cookies on parchment paper with holes cut in them for 12? minutes at 325 degrees.
  • If making as cookie bars, bake at 375 degrees for 31 minutes for a full recipe and 25 minutes for half recipe.

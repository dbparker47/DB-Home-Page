Pretzel Treats
    3 bags of pretzels (how big?)
    2 packages Hidden Valley Ranch Mix
    1 bottle Orville Redenbacher Popping Oil (butter flavor) (How big?)
    1 teaspoon dill
    1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Toss ingredients together.
  • Bake at 200 degrees for 1 hour. Stir every 15 minutes.

Meatless Loaf
    2 cups minced onion
    1 tablespoon butter
    1 pound mushrooms, chopped
    1 1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 teaspoon each basil, thyme, dill
    3-4 medium cloves garlic, minced
    1 1/2 pounds carrots, grated
    2 cup superb bread crumbs
    1 cup packed grated cheddar cheese
    2 eggs, beaten
  • Lightly oil a 9'' x 13'' in baking pan. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  • In a large skillet, saute onions in butter over medium heat for about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms, salt, herbs, and garlic, and continue to saute for about 10 more minutes.
  • In a large bowl, combine carrots, bread crumbs, cheese, and eggs. Add the sauteed mixture and mix well. Spread into prepared pan and sprinkle with bread crumbs. Cover the pan with foil.
  • Bake for 30 minutes covered, then uncover and bake 15 minutes more.
  • Top with sauce (below). Serve hot.
    The Sauce:
    2 c toasted cashews
    1 c water
    2 T minced fresh ginger
    1 T soy sauce
    1 T honey or sugar (brown or white)
    2 T plus 1 t cider vinegar
    1/2 t salt
    cayenne, to taste
  • Puree everything together in a food processor or a blender until it is as smooth as you'd like. Gently heat before serving.

Beef Burgundy
    1/4 cup flour
    2 pounds English roast or chuck roast
    1 onion, sliced
    8 ounces mushrooms
    1/2 cup fresh parsley, minced
    3 garlic cloves
    2 bay leaves
    1 cup Burgundy wine
    1/2 cup beef or vegetable broth
    4-8 carrots
  • Dredge the beef cubes in the flour.
  • Place the beef and remaining ingredients into the Crock Pot Slow Cooker, mix thoroughly and combine.
  • Cover; cook on Low for 4 to 6 hours or on High for 2 to 3 hours.

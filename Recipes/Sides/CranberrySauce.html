Cranberry Sauce
    12 ounces cranberries
    1 cup water or orange juice
    1 cup sugar
  • Boil water.
  • Add sugar, stirring in until dissolved.
  • Add cranberries. Bring to boil, then simmer (open top) for 10 minutes or until cranberries pop.
  • Let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate.

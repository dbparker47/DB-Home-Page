Doughnuts - Cake
    Doughnuts
    1 1/4 cups flour
    1/2 cup sugar
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    1/4 teaspoon baking soda
    Pinch of salt
    2 tablespoons butter, melted
    2/3 cup milk
    1 egg, lightly beaten
    Glaze
    1/3 cup milk
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    2 cups powdered sugar
  • Combine all dry ingredients in one mixing bowl; combine all wet ingredients in another mixing bowl.
  • Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients; mix until just fully incorporated.
  • Cook in doughnut maker.
  • Add milk and vanilla to saucepan and heat until warm. Stir in powdered sugar. Dip doughnuts in glaze while it is still warm.

Sugar Cookies
    3 1/2 cups sifted flour
    1 teaspoon salt
    2 sticks butter, softened (1 cup)
    1 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    Powdered sugar, for rolling out dough
  • Resift flour with the salt.
  • Beat butter and sugar in a medium bowl until creamy. Add egg and vanilla, and beat until blended.
  • Add flour mixture into butter mixture, and mix just enough to combine the ingredients. Refrigerate dough until firm (2 hours?).
  • Roll out some dough until it is 1/8 inch thick. Cut cookie dough into shapes.
  • Bake on ungreased cookie sheet for 10 minutes for people and 8 minutes for candy canes at 375 degrees.
  • For air fryer, place in basket. Bake 3 "people" cookies at 375 degrees for 5-6 minutes.
  • Use icing as in doughnuts.

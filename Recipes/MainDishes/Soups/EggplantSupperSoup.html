Eggplant Supper Soup
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 medium onion, chopped
    1 lb ground beef
    1 eggplant, peeled and diced
    1 clove garlic
    1 cup chopped carrots
    1 cup diced celery
    1 quart tomatoes
    3 1/2 cups beef broth
    1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    1 teaspoon honey
    1/2 teaspoon pepper
    1/2 cup barley
    1 tablespoon dried parsley
    2 cups grated cheese
  • Cook onions, carrots, celery, and garlic patiently on the stove. Add meat and cook.
  • Drain fat and add eggplant, tomatoes, broth, nutmeg, honey, salt, and pepper. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Add barley and simmer 30 minutes more.
  • Serve soup in bowls and top with grated cheese.

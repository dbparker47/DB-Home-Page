Yogurt Biscuits
    2 cups flour
    1/4 teaspoon baking soda
    1/4 cup canola oil
    3 teaspoons baking powder
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 cup plain yogurt
  • In a bowl, sift together dry ingredients. Add oil and yogurt, and mix well.
  • Place dough on a board and knead until smooth. Roll out to 1-inch thinkness and cut round biscuits using a cookie cutter (or make round balls about 2 inches in diameter).
  • Place biscuits on a greased cookie sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes or until brown on top.

Chicken Noodle Soup
    2 T olive oil
    1 onion
    3 garlic cloves
    5-6 carrots
    3+ celery ribs
    1 teaspoon thyme
    1 bay leaf
    2 quarts stock
    8 oz egg noodles
    1 1/2 pounds chicken breasts
    salt to taste
  • Gently sautee onions, carrots celery, and garlic. Cook patiently
  • Put in chicken and cook until done.
  • Shred chicken and put back in. Put everything else in, too.
  • Pour in stock and bring liquid to boil.
  • Simmer for long time
  • Put in noodles 10 minutes before end.

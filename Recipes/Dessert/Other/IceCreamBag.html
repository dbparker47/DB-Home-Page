Ice Cream (Bag)
    One gallon-sized zip bag
    One quart-sized zip bag
    1 cup half & half
    1 tablespoon sugar
    1.5 teaspoons vanilla
    3 cups ice
    1/3 cup kosher/ice cream salt
  • Mix half & half, sugar, and vanilla together. Pour into quart-sized bag, pressing out air. Set aside.
  • Fill gallon-sized bag with ice (roughly half the bag); sprinkle with kosher/ice cream salt; and place the smaller bag in the larger bag. Press out air and seal.
  • With towel or oven mitts, hold the bag and shake for about 10-15 minutes. Check ice cream mixture periodically to make sure it is firming up. If not, add more ice and salt.
  • When ice cream mixture is firm, eat up.

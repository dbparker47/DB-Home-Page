Meat Loaf
    12 ounces ground chuck
    12 ounces ground sirloin
    1 1/2 cups onions, finely chopped
    1 cup rolled oats
    2/3 cup ketchup
    2/3 cup fresh parsley
    3 extra-large eggs, lightly beaten
    1 teaspoon ground thyme
    1 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1/2 teaspoon black pepper (optional)
  • Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Knead mixture with your hands until everything is well blended. Do not overmix.
  • Fill the loaf pan with the meat mixture, mounding the top. Place the pan on a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees until the meat is firm to the touch and has shrunk from the sides of the pan, or until an instant-read thermometer reads 160 degrees. This will take 1 to 1 1/4 hours.
  • Pour off the excess fat and let stand for 15 minutes.

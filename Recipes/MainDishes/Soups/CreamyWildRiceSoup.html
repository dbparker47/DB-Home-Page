Creamy Wild Rice Soup
    1 cup finely chopped onion
    6 tablespoons butter
    1/2 cup flour
    3 cups vegetable broth
    2-3 cups cooked wild rice
    1/2 cup grated carrots
    3 tablespoons slivered almonds
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 cup half and half
    2 tablespoons dry sherry wine
  • In a Dutch oven, saute the onions in butter until they are soft. Add flour, stirring constantly until bubbly.
  • Slowly add the broth (it is easier to avoid lumps if the broth is hot). Add the wild rice, carrots, almonds, and salt. Cook for 10-15 minutes.
  • Add the half and half and sherry. Cook for another 10 minutes.

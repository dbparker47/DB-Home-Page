Stuffed Grape Leaves
    1 recipe Greek Rice Pilaf
    about 24 grape leaves
    10 1/2 silken tofu
    2 tablespoons lemon juice
    1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Preheat oven to $325^\circ$ F. Lightly oil a baking tray.
  • Place each leaf down flat on a clean surface. Add a heaping T of filling near the stem end. Roll tightly, folding in the sides.
  • Arrange the stuffed leaves on the prepared tray. Bake about 20 minutes or until heated through.
  • While the stuffed leaves are baking, place the remaining ingredients in a blender or food processor and whip.
  • Serve the sauce in a side dish.

Tutto Bene Tagliatelle
    1 12 pounds Italian sausage, cooked, chopped, and drained.
    1/4 pound shitake mushrooms, sliced
    1/4 pound oyster mushrooms, sliced
    1/4 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
    1/2 pound sun-dried tomatoes, soaked, drained, and chopped
    2 tablespoons butter
    2-3 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tablespoons parsley, minced
    2 pounds fettucine or Tagliatelle noodles
  • Cook butter and mushrooms over medium heat until mushrooms begin to soften.
  • Add garlic, parsley, sausage, and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until mushrooms are cooked.
  • Cook and drain pasta. Toss pasta in the pan with the remaining ingredients.

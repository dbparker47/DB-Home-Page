Ice Cream (No Churn)
    14 ounce can sweetened condensed milk
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    Pinch of salt
    2 cups heavy cream, cold
    Mix-in options: Cookies, peanut butter cups, Heath bar, Butterfinger
  • Whisk together condensed milk, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl. Set aside.
  • Whip cream in mixer on medium-high speed until firm peaks form (about 2 minutes). Fold about 1 cup of the whipped cream into the condensed milk mixture with rubber spatula until combined.
  • Fold the lightened mixture into the remaining whipped cream until well blended. Pour into a chilled 9x5x3-inch metal loaf pan.
  • Freeze, covered, until thick and creamy (about 2 hours). Swirl in mix-ins with a spoon. Continue to freeze, covered, until solid and scoopable, about 3 hours more.

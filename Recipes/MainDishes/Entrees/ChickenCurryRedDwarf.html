Red Dwarf Chicken Curry
    1 1/2 cups rice
    1 teaspoon turmeric
    1 1/2 pounds chicken, cut into thin strips
    1 onion, finely sliced
    1 tablespoon canola oil
    1 teaspoon curry paste
    2-3 cloves garlic, minced
    4 ounces chicken broth (1 tablespoon "Better than Boullion")
    1 can coconut milk
    1/2 - 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    2-3 tablespoons mango chutney (or peach jam)
  • Cook rice with turmeric.
  • Add onion and chicken to canola oil and brown.
  • Add curry paste, garlic, broth, and coconut milk. Cook until slightly thickened.
  • Add chutney just before serving.

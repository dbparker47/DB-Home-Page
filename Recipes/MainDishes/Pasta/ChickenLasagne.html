Jill Vanderstoep's Chicken Spinach Lasagna
    1/2 cup butter
    2 cloves garlic, chopped
    1/2 cup flour
    1 teaspoon salt (optional)
    2 cups milk
    2 cups chicken broth
    2 cups mozzarella cheese
    1 cup parmesan cheese
    1 onion, chopped
    1/2 teaspoon basil
    1/2 teaspoon oregano
    1/4 teaspoon pepper (optional)
    8 ounces lasagna noodles, uncooked
    2 cups cottage cheese
    2 cups chicken, cooked and cubed
    20 ounces chopped spinach
  • Melt butter in large pan and add garlic.
  • Stir in flour and salt. Add milk and broth and whisk to prevent lumps. Heat until it boils, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 minute.
  • Stir in mozzarella cheese and 1/2 cup parmesan cheese. Add onion, basil, oregano, and pepper. Stir constantly over low heat until cheese melts.
  • Spread 1/4 of the cheese sauce (from above) in a 9'' x 13'' ungreased pan. Top with 3 uncooked noodles.
  • Spread half of the cottage cheese, 1/4 of the cheese sauce, and top with 3 uncooked noodles.
  • Spread the remaining cottage cheese; layer the chicken; put spinach over chicken; spread 1/4 of the cheese sauce. Top with 3 uncooked noodles.
  • Spread remaining cheese sauce, and top with 1/2 cup parmesan cheese.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes until bubbly and starting to brown. Let stand for 10-15 minutes before serving.

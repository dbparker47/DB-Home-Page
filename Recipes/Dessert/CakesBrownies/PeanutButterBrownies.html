Peanut Butter Brownies
    ½ cup peanut butter
    ⅓ cup butter, softened
    ⅔ cup white sugar
    ½ cup packed brown sugar
    2 large eggs
    ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
    1 cup all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    ¼ teaspoon salt
    1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • Cream together peanut butter and butter in a medium bowl. Gradually blend in white sugar, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla; mix until fluffy.
  • Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl. Stir flour mixture and chocolate chips into the peanut butter mixture until well blended.
  • Spread mixture into 9" x 9" baking pan. Bake at 350 degrees until the top springs back when touched, 35 minutes without chocolate chips, 40 minutes with chocolate chips. Cool and cut into 16 squares.

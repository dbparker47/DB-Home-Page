Zuccanoes
    4 medium sized zucchini (about 7 inches long)
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 large onion, diced
    1 teaspoon salt
    1/2 lb mushrooms, minced
    6 medium cloves garlic, minced
    1 1/2 cups cooked brown rice
    1 1/2 cups finely minced almonds
    3 tablespoons lemon juice
    grated swiss cheese
    small handfuls of freshly minced herbs if available (parsley, basil, thyme, dill, chives)
  • Cut the zucchini lengthwise down the middle. Use a spoon to scoop out the insides, leaving a canoe with 1/4 inch shell. Finely mince the scrapings; set the canoes aside.
  • Heat the olive oil in a medium size skillet. Add onion and salt, and saute over medium heat until the onion is soft.
  • Add the zucchini innards, garlic, and mushrooms, and saute another 10 minutes.
  • Place rice and almonds in a medium sized bowl. Stir in the saute and lemon juice and mix well. Season to taste with optional herbs.
  • Fill the zucchini shells, top with cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes until heated through. Serve hot.

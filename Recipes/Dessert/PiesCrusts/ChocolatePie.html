Chocolate Pie
    2 squares unsweetened chocolate
    2 tablespoons butter
    1/3 cup flour
    3/4 cup sugar
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    2 1/2 cups milk, scalded
    3 eggs, separated
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Melt chocolate and butter over hot water (double boiler)
  • Mix flour, sugar, and salt and stir into chocolate. Add milk and stir constantly until mixture is fully thickened (about 15 minutes).
  • Beat egg yolks well and stir a little of the chocolate mixture into the egg yolks. Pour the egg mixture into the rest of the hot mixture and cook 2 minutes stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  • Cool partially and stir in vanilla. pour into pie shell (regular, graham cracker, or chocolate).
  • Cooking hint: Pour a little of the hot chocolate mixture at a time into the egg so the egg doesn't cook and harden. Do it a few times. Then pour the egg mixture into the hot chocolate mixture, stirring well.

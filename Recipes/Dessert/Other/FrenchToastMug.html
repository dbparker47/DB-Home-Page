French Toast (Mug)
    2 slices bread, cubed
    Raspberries
    1 egg, beaten
    1/3 cup milk
    1 tablespoon cocoa powder
    1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    1/2 vanilla extract
  • Place half the bread in a 16-oz mug. Layer in half the raspberries, the rest of the break, and the rest of the raspberries.
  • Separately, whisk together egg, mild, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and vanilla extract. Pour mixture into mug and let sit 1 minute.
  • Microwave for about 2 minutes. Top with maple syrup and nuts.

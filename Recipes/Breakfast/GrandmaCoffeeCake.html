Grandma's Coffee Cake (Louise)
    Cake:
    1/2 cup butter
    1 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    2 cups flour
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1 1/2 cup plain yogurt or sour cream
    1 tablespoon vanilla
    Topping:
    1 cup brown sugar
    1 heaping tablespoon flour
    2 tablspoons melted butter
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Cream the sugar and butter.
  • Add the eggs; add all dry ingredients; add yogurt and vanilla; mix well.
  • Grease and flour bundt pan.
  • Mix the topping ingredients together; sprinkle some topping in bundt pan.
  • Alternate pouring batter and sprinkling topping in bundt pan.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.

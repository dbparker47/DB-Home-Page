Tomato Rice Soup
    1 onion
    3 cloves garlic
    2 carrots
    2 stalks celery
    2 quarts tomatoes with juice
    1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
    1+ tablespoon honey
    1 cup brown rice
    4 cups broth
    1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Gently sautee onions, carrots celery, and garlic. Cook patiently
  • Meanwhile, cook rice most of the way.
  • Put vegetables in food processor, along with all or most of tomatoes, cayenne pepper, and honey.
  • Return everything to the pot and add rice and broth. Bring to boil and simmer for 30 minutes.
  • Add lemon juice and serve.

Zucchini, Tomato, and Swiss Cheese Pie
    1 tablespoon unsalted butter
    1/4 cup bread crumbs
    1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 medium onion, diced
    2 garlic cloves, minced
    2 medium tomatoes, diced
    3 medium zucchini, quartered and sliced
    1/2 teaspoon fennel seed, crushed
    1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
    black pepper (optional)
    3 eggs
    1/3 cup milk
    1/4 pound swiss cheese, grated
    3 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
  • Grease pie plate with 1/2 teaspoon butter. Sprinkle with bread crumbs, covering the bottom and sides.
  • Saute oil, onion, and garlic for 10 minutes. Stir in tomatoes and saute for 5 more minutes. Raise heat to high, add zucchini, fennel seed, salt, and pepper. Cook approximately 5 minutes more. Remove from heat and cool for 5 minutes.
  • Beat eggs, stir in milk, and add zucchini mixture. Pour half of mixture into a pie plate, top with cheese, and then pour on the rest of the mixture. Top with parmesan cheese, and dot with butter.
  • Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.

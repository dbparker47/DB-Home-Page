Black Beans and Rice
    Olive oil
    1 onion
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 cup brown rice
    2 cups vegetable broth
    1 teaspoon ground cumin
    1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • In a pot, cook olive oil, onion, and garlic.
  • Add rice and sautee for two minutes.
  • Add broth, bring to a boil, and lower heat until rice is cooked.
  • Add spices and beans, and heat through.

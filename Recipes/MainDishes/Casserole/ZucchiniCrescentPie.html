Zucchini Crescent Pie
    4 cups thinly sliced zucchini
    1 cup chopped onion
    1/4-1/2 cup butter
    1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped (2 tablespoons dried)
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon pepper
    1/4 teaspoon garlic powder (or one clove)
    1/4 teaspoon basil
    1/4 teaspoon oregano
    2 eggs, beaten
    8 ounces mozarella or muenster cheese, shredded
    8 ounce can crescent dinner rolls
    2 tablespoons mustard
  • Cook and stir zucchini, onion, and butter for 10 minutes. Stir in parsley, salt, pepper, garlic, basil, and oregano.
  • Combine eggs and cheese. Stir in zucchini mixture.
  • Separate dinner rolls into 8 triangles, and place in an ungreased 10-inch pie pan. Press over bottom and up sides of the pan to form a crust. Spread crust with mustard.
  • Pour vegetable mixture into crust. Bake at $375^\circ$ for 18--20 minutes or until center is set. Let stand 10 minutes.

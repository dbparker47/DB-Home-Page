Grandma's Bubble Bread (Joy)
    Dough:
    2/3 cup sugar
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
    4 tubes of biscuits (32 oz total)
    Glaze:
    12 tablespoons (1 1/2 sticks) butter, melted
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/2 cup brown sugar
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Cut biscuit tubes in fourths with pizza cutter or shears.
  • Mix cinnamon and sugar; roll biscuits in cinnamon/sugar mixture.
  • Layer biscuit pieces in a Bundt pan or angel food cake pan.
  • Mix glaze ingredients together and pour over biscuits.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.

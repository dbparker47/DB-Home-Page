Baked Artichoke Dip
    2 cups mayonaise
    2 cups grated parmesan cheese
    28 ounces artichoke hearts
  • Chop the artichoke hearts into small pieces. Put into a baking dish.
  • Mix in the mayo and cheese.
  • Bake at 350 degrees until hot and bubbly (about 40 minutes).

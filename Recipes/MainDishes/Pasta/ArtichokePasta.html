Artichoke Pasta
    14 ounces artichoke hearts
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 tablespoon butter
    1 cup sliced onions
    1 tablespoon dried basil
    1 cup plain yogurt
    16 ounces cottage cheese
    2 pounds pasta (linguine or spaghetti)
    salt, pepper, cayenne, parmesan cheese - to taste
  • Drain the artichoke hearts and cut them up into small pieces.
  • Heat the olive oil and butter in a skillet. Add the onions and saute them until soft. Add the artichokes hearts and basil. Saute 5 minutes more.
  • Remove from heat. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Cook and drain the pasta, and toss immediately with the still-warm sauce.

Lemon Bars
    2 cups sifted all-purpose flour
    1 cup confectioners' sugar
    1 cup butter, melted
    4 eggs
    2 cups white sugar
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    ¼ cup all-purpose flour
    ⅝ cup lemon juice
  • In a medium bowl, stir together 2 cups flour and confectioners' sugar. Blend in the melted butter. Press into the bottom of a greased 9" x 13" pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes, or until golden.
  • In a large bowl, beat eggs until light. Combine the sugar, baking powder and 1/4 cup of flour so there will be no flour lumps. Stir the sugar mixture into the eggs. Finally, stir in the lemon juice. Pour over the prepared crust.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 32? minutes. Allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.

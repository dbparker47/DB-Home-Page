Grandma's Apple Pie (Joy)
    Regular Pie Crust
    8-9 Tart Apples
    1 cup sugar
    1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
    Dots of butter
  • Break crust into two pieces. Flatten each with a rolling pin. Put one into pie pan.
  • Put apples on crust in pie pan. Mix flour, sugar, and cinnamon together and pour over apples. Dot with butter.
  • Bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes with aluminum foil around the edge of the crust. Bake 25 minutes more without aluminum foil.

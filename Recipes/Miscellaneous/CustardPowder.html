Custard Powder
    1 cup milk powder
    1/2 cup corn starch
    1 1/2 tablespoons vanilla sugar
  • Add the milk powder, corn starch, and vanilla to a fine mesh sieve over a large bowl. Whisk well to combine.
  • Transfer to an airtight container and store in the pantry.

Used in: Nanaimo Bars

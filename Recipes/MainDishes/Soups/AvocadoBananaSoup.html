Avocado-Banana Soup
    2 ripe avocados
    2 medium bananas
    21 ounces condensed chicken broth (condensed?)
    3 cups half-and-half
    1/2 cup dry white vermouth or white wine or grape juice
    1/4 cup lime juice (from 2 limes)
    1 tablespoon fresh grated ginger root
    2 tablespoons sugar
    1/2-1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce (optional)
    Salt
    8 ounces shredded crab (optional)
  • Halve, pit, and peel the avocados. Puree avocados and bananas with half of the chicken broth in a food processor or blender. Whip until the mixture is smooth. Pour into a Dutch oven.
  • Add half-and-half, vermouth, lime juice, ginger, sugar, hot pepper sauce, salt, and crab. Stir until well mixed.
  • Chill or gently heat through, stirring occasionally.

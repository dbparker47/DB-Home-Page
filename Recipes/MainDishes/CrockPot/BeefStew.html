Beef Stew
    2+ lb English roast or chuck roast
    2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    1/4-1/2 cup flour
    1 teaspoon paprika
    1 1/2 cups beef or vegetable broth
    1 garlic clove, minced
    1 bay leaf
    4-8 carrots sliced (there cannot be too many carrots)
    1 large onion
    1-3 ribs of celery, sliced
    3 potatoes, diced
  • Place meat in the crockpot.
  • Combine flour and paprika, and stir into meat until the meat is coated thoroughly.
  • Add remaining ingredients and mix well.
  • Cover and cook on low for 10-12 hours.

Cream Cheese Frosting
    3 oz. (small package) creamed cheese
    4 tablespoons butterr
    3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    Some milk (but not too much)
  • Stir together ingredients in a bowl.

Used in: Grandma's Cinnamon Rolls (Joy)

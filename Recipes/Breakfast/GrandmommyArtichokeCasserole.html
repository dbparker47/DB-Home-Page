Grandmommy's Artichoke Breakfast Casserole (Hamo)
    6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
    1/2 cup bread crumbs
    2 onions, finely chopped
    9 eggs, separated
    30 ounces artichoke hearts
    1/4 teaspoon cayenne
    1 pound sharp cheddar cheese
    2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Mix 2 tablespoons of the butter with the bread crumbs?
  • Cook onions with the remaining butter.
  • Beat egg whites until fluffy, and beat yolks until foamy.
  • Mix everything in a large bowl., and pour ungreased into a 9'' x 13'' x 2'' baking dish. Store overnight.
  • Top with bread crumbs, and bake at 350 degrees for 35--40 minutes.

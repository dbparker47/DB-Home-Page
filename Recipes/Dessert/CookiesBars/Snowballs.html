Snowballs
    1 lb. butter
    1/2 cup sugar
    4 cups flour (add 1/2 cup flour for no nuts)
    2 cups walnuts
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    2 teaspoons vanilla
    Powdered sugar
  • Melt butter on stove; let it cool a little.
  • Add flour, salt, and sugar; mix well.
  • Stir in nuts and vanilla, and roll into balls.
  • Place on cookie sheet; bake at 400 degrees for 10-15 minutes. Snowballs should be slightly browned on the edges.
  • Let them cool a little; roll in powdered sugar.

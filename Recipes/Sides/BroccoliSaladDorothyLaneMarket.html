Dorothy Lane Market Broccoli Salad
    Salad:
    2 bunches broccoli, washed, trimmed, and cut into flowerettes\\ 1 cup raisins
    1/2 cup chopped onion
    10-15 slices bacon, cooked crisp, drained, and crumbled
    Dressing:
    1 cup mayonnaise
    2 tablespoons vinegar
    1/2 cup sugar
  • Toss together broccoli, raisins, onion, and bacon.
  • Combine dressing ingredients and pour over salad. Do not stir.
  • Refrigerate, covered, overnight. Mix well before serving.

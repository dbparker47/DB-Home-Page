Sourdough Pancakes & Waffles
    1/2 cup butter
    1 cup milk
    1 cup sourdough starter
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 tablespoon brown sugar
    1 1/2 cups flour
    2 eggs
    1/4 teaspoon baking soda
    Other stuff, like oats, cornmeal, and liquid to compensate?
    chocolate chips or blueberries (optional)
  • Heat butter and milk in a pan until butter melts. Bring to room temperature.
  • Mix starter, salt, brown sugar, flour, and butter/milk mixture and mix to form a thick batter. Let it stand at room temperature for 8-14 hours.
  • Mix in eggs and baking soda.
  • Cook on the griddle or waffle iron.
  • Makes about 13 waffles or 11 pancakes. Warning: Batter is really thick.

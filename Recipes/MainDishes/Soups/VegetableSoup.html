Vegetable Soup
    2 T olive oil
    1 onion
    5-6 carrots
    3+ celery ribs
    1 teaspoon oregano
    1 teaspoon basil
    1/2 teaspoon thyme
    28 oz diced tomatoes
    2 quarts stock
    1 cup egg noodles
    Lots of vegetables (corn, green beans lima beans, peas, potatoes, zucchini, etc.)
  • Gently sautee onions, then carrots and celery, then garlic. Cook patiently.
  • Add in broth and tomatoes. Bring to a boil. Bring down to simmer.
  • Add in vegetables. Simmer for 30-40 minutes.
  • Put in pasta 20 minutes before end.

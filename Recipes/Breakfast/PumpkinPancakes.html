Pumpkin Pancakes
    1 cup flour
    3 tablespoons sugar
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
    1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
    1 egg
    1 cup plain yogurt
    1/4 cup pumpkin puree
    2 tablespoons butter, melted and cooled, plus 1 tablespoon for the skillet
  • In a bowl, stir together all dry ingredients.
  • In a large bowl, beat the egg for 30 seconds. Add yogurt, pumpkin puree, and 2 tablespoons melted butter. Beat well.
  • Stir in dry ingredients, and beat just until combined.
  • Grease skillet with butter (or not). Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake. Flip with spatula when tops bubble and edges are slightly dry. Cook until other side is golden brown (about 2 minutes per side).

Peeny Butter Fudge
    1 cup milk
    2 oz unsweetened baking chocolate
    1 tablespoon butter
    2 cups sugar
    1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
    1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • Combine milk, chocolate, and butter in saucepan. Stir over low heat with wooden spoon until squares begin to melt or have melted completely.
  • Add sugar and stir. Increase heat to medium and let cook until mixture is at a low boil (stirring occasionally). Let boil for five minutes. Test by putting drops of mixture in glass of cold water until drops look like tadpoles.
  • Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla. Place saucepan in cold water until bottom is warm.
  • Stir in peanut butter, and beat mixture until it is not shiny. Pour into greased 8x8 baking pan and let cool. Cut into ? pieces.

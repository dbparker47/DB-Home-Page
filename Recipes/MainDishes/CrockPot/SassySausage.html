Sassy Sausage
    30 ounces black beans
    1 pound frozen corn
    1 quart canned tomatoes
    2 cups wild rice
    1/2 teaspoon chile powder
    1/2 teaspoon cumin
    1 clove garlic
    2 sausages
  • Toss it all into the crockpot. Cook on low for a long time.

Evaporated Milk
  • Decide how much evaporated milk you want.
  • Multiply that amount by 2.25. This is how much regular milk you need.
  • Gently boil milk until you have the desired amount. Set stove at 5.5 and stir constantly to maximize evaporation and minimize milk temperature.
    Used in: Chocolate Fudge

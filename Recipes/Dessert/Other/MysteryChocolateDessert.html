Mystery Chocolate Dessert
    1 1/4 cup sugar
    1/2 cup baking cocoa
    2 tablespoons flour
    1/8 teaspoon salt
    3/4 cup water
    3/4 cup + 1 tablespoon semisweet chocolate chips
    1 tablespoon brewed coffee (optional)
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    2 eggs
    1 egg white
    10 fresh strawberry halves (optional)
  • In a small saucepan, combine 3/4 cup sugar, cocoa, flour, and salt. Gradually stir in water. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from heat; Stir in the chocolate chips, coffee and vanilla until smooth. Transfer to a large bowl.
  • In a mother bowl, beat eggs and egg white until slightly thickened. Gradually add remaining sugar, beating until thick and lemon-colored. Fold into chocolate mixture.
  • Transfer to ten 4-oz ramekins coated with cooking spray. Place ramekins in a baking pan; add 1 in of boiling water to pan. Bake, uncovered at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes or just until centers are set. Garnish with strawberry halves if desired. Server immediately. Yield: 10 servings.

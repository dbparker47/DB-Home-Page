Chocolate Chip Cookie (Mug)
    1 tablespoon butter
    Pinch of salt
    1 tablespoon each brown/white sugar
    1 egg
    1/2 teaspoons vanilla
    2 heaping tablespoons flour
    1 tablespoons chocolate chips
  • Melt butter. Add sugar, vanilla, and egg. Mix together. Add flour and stir. Add chocolate chips and stir. Microwave for 50 seconds on half power (one cookie).
  • Microwave for 50 seconds.

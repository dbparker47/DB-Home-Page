Wet Burritos
    Innards:
    1 pound lean ground beef
    1/2 cup chopped onion
    1 clove garlic, minced
    2 teaspoons chili powder
    1 teaspoon dried oregano
    1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/4 teaspoon pepper
    15 ounces black beans
    Sauce:
    2 tablespoons butter
    3 tablespoons flour
    1 3/4 cup water
    8 ounces tomato sauce
    1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
    Boullion
    3/4 teaspoon cumin
  • In a large skillet cook ground beef, onion, and garlic. Drain well.
  • Add chili powder, oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper; simmer for 5 minutes. Add beans and 1/2 cup of the sauce (see below). Cook until heated through; keep warm.
  • For sauce, melt the butter and stir in the flour until smooth. Stir in the water and bring to a boil until the sauce thickens.
  • Add tomato sauce, chili powder, boullion, and cumin. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Wrap tortillas in foil; bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes, or until thoroughly heated. Spoon about 1/2 cup ground beef mixture on each warm tortilla. Roll tightly and place seam-side down on plate. When all burritos are made, spoon remaining sauce over the burritos.

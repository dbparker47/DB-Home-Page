Omelets
    12 extra-large eggs
    1/2 cup milk
    Cheddar, Tomato, Lightly Sauteed Mushrooms, etc.
  • Beat eggs and milk together.
  • Heat small pan. Put a small amount of canola oil in pan.
  • Pour 1/2 cup of egg mixture into the pan.
  • Immediately place tomatoes and mushrooms in omelet.
  • After draining top wet part to underneath the omelet, place cheese in the omelet. Cook until cheese melts and omelet is dryish on top.
  • Fold omelet and put on plate.

Bow-tie Pasta with Tomatoes, Basil, and Balsamic Vinegar
    1 pound bow-tie pasta
    1 clove garlic, minced
    1 package grape tomatoes, quartered
    1/4 cup fresh basil (or 3 teaspoons dried basil)
    1/4 cup fresh parsley (or 3 teaspoons dried parsley)
    1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    1/4-1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    2 tablespoons lemon juice
    2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente. Drain pasta.
  • Meanwhile, combine the rest of the ingredients in a large pasta bowl.
  • Add the pasta and serve immediately.

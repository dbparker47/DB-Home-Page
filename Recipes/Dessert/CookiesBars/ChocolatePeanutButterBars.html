Chocolate Peanut Butter Bars
    2 cups graham cracker crumbs
    2 cups confectioners' sugar
    1 cup butter, melted
    1 cup peanut butter
    1 Â½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
    4 tablespoons (1/4 cup) peanut butter
  • Mix together graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, butter, and 1 cup peanut butter in a medium bowl until well-blended. Press evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13-inch pan. Place pan in refrigerator
  • Microwave chocolate chips at half power until completely melted, stopping every 30-60 seconds to stir. Stir remaining peanut butter.
  • Spread chocolate mixture over peanut butter mixture. Refrigerate for about 45 minutes before cutting into 18 squares. Do not refrigerate too long, or chocolate will crack and separate from peanut butter. Store in air-tight container in refrigerator.

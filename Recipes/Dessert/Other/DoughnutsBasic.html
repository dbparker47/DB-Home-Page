Doughnuts - Basic
    1 1/2 cups flour
    1/2 cup sugar
    1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
    9 tablespoons butter, melted
    3/4 cup milk
    1 egg, lightly beaten
  • Combine all dry ingredients in one mixing bowl; combine all wet ingredients in another mixing bowl.
  • Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients; mix until batter is smooth.
  • Cook in doughnut maker.

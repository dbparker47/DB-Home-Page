Cranberry Squash Pasta Salad
    5 cups bow-tie pasta
    4 cups baked butternut squash
    1/2 pound cooked sausage
    4 cups sliced mushrooms, sauteed
    1/4 cup dried cranberries
    1/4 cup chopped fresh sage
    2 tablespoons walnut oil
    1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
    2 cloves garlic, finely minced
    1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Boil pasta.
  • Add squash, sausage, mushrooms, cranberries, and sage.
  • Mix together oil, vinegar, garlic, pepper, and pepper flakes as a dressing. Pour over pasta and toss.

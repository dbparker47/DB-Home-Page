Chocolate Upside-Down Pudding Cake
    Cake
    1 cup flour
    1 tablespoon baking powder
    1/4 cup cocoa powder
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/2 cup butter
    1/2 cup milk
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    Sauce:
    3/4 cup sugar
    1/4 cup cocoa powder
    2 cups boiling water
  • Mix all cake ingredients together until ``just mixed''. Spread in a lightly greased 8'' x 8'' pan.
  • In a medium bowl, mix all sauce ingredients together, except the water. Sprinkle evenly on top of the cake mixture.
  • Carefully pour water over the top. Do not mix.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.

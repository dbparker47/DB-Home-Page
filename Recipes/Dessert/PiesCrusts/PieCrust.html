Regular Pie Crust
    2/3 cup canola oil
    2 cups all-purpose flour
    1/3 cup water
  • Mix oil and water briskly.
  • Dump oil-water mixture on flour and mix vigorously with fork. Bring crust together with hands. Pray for good luck.
  • If you need to blind bake it, bake crust 8-10 minutes at 475 degrees.
    Used in: Cheese & Bacon Quiche, Spinach Ricotta Pie, Blueberry Pie, Grandma's Apple Pie (Joy), Rhubarb Pie, Squash Pie, and Sweet Potato Pie

Squash Pie
    1 large baked butternut squash (or 29 ounce can of pumpkin)
    Half-recipe of Regular Pie Crust
    3 eggs
    1 1/2 cups coconut milk, heavy cream, or evaporated milk
    1/2 cup sugar
    1/3 cup packed brown sugar
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
    1 teaspoon ground or grated fresh ginger
    1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
    1/8 teaspoon ground cloves or allspice
    1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Mix together filling ingredients (i.e. everything from the eggs to the salt).
  • Pour filling into crust.
  • Bake at 400 degrees for 75 minutes, or until center of pie barely jiggles when you shake it.

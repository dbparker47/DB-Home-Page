Half-Marathon Pasta
    1/2 pound chicken breast
    Enough spinach
    Onion, chopped
    1-2 cloves garlic
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    Pasta
  • Cook chicken and boil pasta.
  • At the same time, cook onion and garlic in olive oil.
  • After onion and garlic are sufficiently cooked, add spinach. Cook until it wilts.
  • Add chicken and pasta. Toss together and serve..

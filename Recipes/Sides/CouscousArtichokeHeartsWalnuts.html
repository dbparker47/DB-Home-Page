Couscous with Artichoke Hearts and Walnuts
    1 cup boiling water
    1 1/2 cup couscous (uncooked)
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 teaspoon salt
    14 ounces artichoke hearts, chopped
    1/2 cup minced scallions
    1 large clove of garlic, minced
    1 cup chopped fresh parsley
    1-2 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
    1 tablespoon fresh chopped mint (or 1 teaspoon dried mint)
    3 tablsepoon olive oil
    1/2-1 cup lemon juice (to taste)
    1/2 chopped walnuts
    (optional) goat cheese
  • Put the couscous in a large heat proof bowl. Add the boiling water, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt. Let it sit for 5 minutes and then fluff with a fork.
  • Mix in the artichoke hearts, scallions, garlic, parsley, mint and dill.
  • Stir in the oil, lemon juice, and walnuts. Top with goat cheese.
  • Serve at room temperature or chilled.

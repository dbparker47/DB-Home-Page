Rhubarb Upside-Down Cake
    Part A:
    2/3 cup boiling water
    1/2 cup oats
    2 tablespoons butter
    1/2 cup sugar
    2 cups rhubarb, diced
    Part B:
    1 cup flour
    1 teaspoon baking powder
    1/4 teaspoon baking soda
    1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    Part C:
    2/3 cup sugar
    1/2 cup brown sugar
    1/4 cup cooking oil
    1 egg
  • Melt butter in an 8'' x 8'' x 2'' pan.
  • Stir in the sugar from Part A, and sprinkle in rhubarb.
  • Mix together the Part B ingredients, and set aside.
  • Mix together the Part C ingredients. Add water and oats.
  • Add Part B ingredients to this mixture, and pour over the rhubarb mixture.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes.

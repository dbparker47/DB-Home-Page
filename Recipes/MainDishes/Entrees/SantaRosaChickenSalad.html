Santa Rosa Chicken Salad
    1 cup black rice
    1/4 cup lemon juice
    3 chicken breasts, cooked and cubed
    4 green onions, finely sliced
    1 red bell pepper, diced (or 2)
    3 ounces pea pods, trimmed
    2 medium avocados, diced
    1 cup pecans, chopped and toasted
    Vinagrette Dressing (with rice vinegar and canola oil)
  • Early in the day, prepare rice, chicken, onions, bell pepper, and pea pods. These can then be combined or just placed in a bowl together to toss before serving. Cover and refrigerate.
  • Chop and toast the pecans and place them in a ziplock bag to add just before serving.
  • Just before serving, add the avocados, lemon juice, and dressing. Sprinkle with pecans, or serve pecans on the side.

Mediterranean Lemon Soup
    2 tablespoon butter
    1 cup finely-minced onion
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 cup cooked brown rice
    1/2 cup lemon juice
    2 cup vegetable stock
    2 beaten eggs (at room temp)
    1 teaspoon dried crushed mint
  • In a soup sized pan, saute the onion in butter with salt and pepper until the onion is soft and translucent.
  • Add the rice, stock, and lemon juice. Heat is covered, over a low flame until it boils gently.
  • Stir the soup rapidly as you drizzle in the beaten egg. Add mint; remove from heat.

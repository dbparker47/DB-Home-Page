Senator Russell Sweet Potatoes
    Casserole
    3 cups canned sweet potatoes or baked butternut squash, mashed
    1 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1/2 cup evaporated milk
    1/2 cup butter, melted
    Topping:
    1 cup brown sugar
    1 cup pecans, chopped
    1/2 cup flour
    1/2 cup butter, partly melted
  • Mix casserole ingredients with a mixer until smooth. Pour into a buttered 9'' x 13'' baking dish.
  • Mix topping ingredients by hand to form a crumb mixture. Sprinkle and pat on top of sweet potatoes.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Chinese Noodle Soup
    Broth
    6 cups chicken broth
    4 garlic cloves, smashed
    1" piece of ginger, sliced
    3 teaspoons soy sauce
    3 teaspoons chinese cooking wine
    4 teaspoons honey
    1 teaspoon sesame oil
    Toppings & Noodles
    6 oz chinese egg noodles
    1 large bok choy
    Other vegetables (e.g. green onions, carrots, broccoli, bean sprouts, zucchini, cabbage)
    1 1/2 pound chicken
  • Place broth ingredients and carrots in a saucepan over high heat. Place lid on, bring to simmer then reduce to medium high. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • At the same time, prepare noodles. Use sesame oil to prevent clumping.
  • Chop vegetables, remove garlic and ginger, and cook vegetables in broth (slow-cooking vegetables go in first). Cook bok choy last (1 minute).
  • Place noodles in bowls, and pour soup over noodles.

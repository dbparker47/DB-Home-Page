Bulghur Salad with Corn Zucchini, and Shredded Basil
    1 1/2 cups bulghur
    1 1/2 cups frozen corn kernels, thawed
    1 tomato, cut into small cubes
    1 cup thinly sliced zucchini rounds, cut into sixths
    3 tablespoons finely shredded fresh basil
    1/4 cup thinly silvered red onion \\ 1/3 cup olive oil
    1/3 cup lemon juice
    2 cloves minced garlic
    1/2 t salt
  • Place the bulghur in a large bowl. Pour two cups of boiling water and let it soak for 30 minutes until tender. If there is any liquid not soaked up, drain it.
  • Stir in the corn, tomato, zucchini, basil, and onion. In a separate bowl beat together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and salt. Pour it over the bulghur and mix well. Let sit 30 minutes or cover and chill for up to 24 hours, then bring to room temperature before serving.

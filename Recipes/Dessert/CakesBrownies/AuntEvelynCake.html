Aunt Evelyn's Chocolate Sheet Cake
    Cake
    1/2 cup butter
    1/2 cup oil
    4 tablespoons cocoa (30 gm)
    1 cup water
    2 cups flour (250 gm)
    2 cups sugar (400 gm)
    2 eggs, beaten
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1 teaspoon cinnamon
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    1/2 cup buttermilk or clabbered milk
    Frosting:
    1/2 cup butter
    6 tablespoons milk
    4 tablespoons cocoa (30 gm)
    3 1/2 to 4 cups powdered sugar (480 gm)
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1 cup nuts (optional)
  • For the cake, bring butter, oil, cocoa, and water to a boil. Pour over the sugar and flour, and blend well.
  • Add the eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, baking soda, and milk. Blend well.
  • Place in a greased 9'' x 13'' pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out dry.
  • For the frosting, melt the butter and mix in the milk and cocoa well. Bring to a rapid boil and remove from heat.
  • Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and nuts. Spread on cake as soon as it comes out of the oven.

Pasta Alfredo
    2 tablespoons butter
    2 cups heavy cream or milk
    4 cloves garlic
    4 cups broth
    1 pound fettucine (or other suitable pasta)
  • Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium low heat. Sautee garlic.
  • Add cream, broth, and pasta.
  • Simmer over medium heat (stirring occasionally) until pasta cooks and sauce thickens (about 20 minutes).

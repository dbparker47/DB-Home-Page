Grandma Hazel's Biscuits
    2 cups flour
    1 tablespoon baking powder
    1 teaspoon salt
    1/4 cup shortening
    2/3-3/4 cup milk or buttermilk
  • Combine flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  • With a fork, blend in just enough milk until dough leaves the sides of the bowl.
  • Knead gently 2-3 times on a lightly floured surface.
  • Roll dough to 1/2-inch thickness, and cut biscuits without twisting the biscuit cutter.
  • Spray baking sheet with cooking spray, and place biscuits on the baking sheets, 1 inch apart for crisp-sided biscuits or almost touching for softer sided biscuits.
  • Bake at 500 degrees for 8-10 minutes.

Spanish Rice Burritos
    12 flour tortillas
    14 oz can of diced tomatoes
    1 1/2 cup brown rice
    2 1/2 cup water
    15 oz can of black beans
    1/2 lb ground round (optional)
    1/2 cup salsa
    1/2 teaspoon honey
    1 clove garlic, minced
    1/8 teaspoon pepper (optional)
    1 medium onion, chopped
    1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
    cheddar cheese
    yogurt
    green onions
  • Cook the onions, garlic, and meat in a small saut√© pan. Set aside.
  • Combine rice and water in a medium pot and cook covered for 20 minutes.
  • Add the diced tomatoes, salt, pepper, and oregano. Cook for about 15 minutes.
  • Stir in the black beans, meat, onion, and garlic, and heat through.
  • To serve, place some of the mixture on a tortilla and sprinkle cheese on it. Top with green onions and yogurt.

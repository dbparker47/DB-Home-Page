Zucchini Feta Pancakes
    4 eggs, separated (yolks optional)
    4 packed cups coarsely grated zucchini
    1 cup finely crumbled feta cheese
    1/2 cup finely minced onions
    1 teaspoon dried mint (or 1 T fresh, finely minced mint)
    a little salt
    1/3 cup flour
    oil for frying
    plain yogurt for topping
  • Beat the egg whites until stiff.
  • In a medium sized bowl, combine the zucchini, egg yolks, feta, scallions, seasonings, and flour. Mix well.
  • Fold the egg whites into the zucchini mixture.
  • Heat a little oil in a heavy skillet. When it is very hot, add spoonfuls of batter, and fry on both sides until golden and crisp.
  • Serve immediately, topped with yogurt.

Spaghetti Sauce
    1 onion
    2 garlic cloves
    1/2 pound ground round
    4 ounces mushrooms
    1 green bell pepper
    6 ounces tomato paste
    14 ounces diced tomatoes
    1/2 teaspoon oregano
    1/2 teaspoon thyme
    1/2 teaspoon fennel
    1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Cook onion and garlic until onion is soft.
  • Add beef and cook a while.
  • Add bell pepper and mushrooms. Cook until mushrooms just start to turn color.
  • Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and spices. Simmer for 30 minutes.

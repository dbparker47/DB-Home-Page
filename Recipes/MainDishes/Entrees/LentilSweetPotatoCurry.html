Lentil Sweet Potato Curry
    1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
    3 cups water, divided
    1 large onion, chopped
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    2 medium carrots, chopped
    3 garlic cloves, minced
    2 medium sweet potatoes, chopped
    4 teaspoons curry powder
    3/4 teaspoon salt
    1/2 teaspoon pepper
    2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    1 medium apple, chopped
    1 tablespoon brown sugar
    3 cups hot cooked brown rice
  • In a small saucepan, combine lentils and 2 cups water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until tender. Drain.
  • Meanwhile, in a large saucepan, saut√© onion in oil until tender. Addo carrots and garlic; cook 2 minutes longer. Sitir in sweet potatoes, curry, salt, pepper, and remaining water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Add tomatoes, apple, and brown sugar; cover and cook 10-15 minutes or until apple is tender. Stir in lentils; heat through. Serve with rice.

Dribbling Drills

You can see these on the First Touch Training YouTube Channel

  • Foundation
  • Dribble Cuts: This can be done with or without cones (approximately 10) placed a couple feet away from one another. Players should work toward doing the drill without looking at the ball. Players move ball back and forth with their feet, using the following patterns.
    • Inside to inside: Footwork for trapping: L-DR-LR-TL-R-DL-RL-TR (repeat). Footwork for no trapping: (L-)DR-LR-DL-RL (repeat)
    • Outside to outside: Footwork for trapping: L-DOR-LR-TOL-R-DOL-RL-TOR (repeat). Footwork for no trapping: (L-)DOR-LR-DOL-RL.
    • Inside to outside (same foot): Footwork for trapping (right foot): (L-)DOR-L-TR-L-DR-LR-DOL-R-TL-R-DL-RL (repeat). Footwork for no trapping (right foot): (L-)DOR-L-DIR-L (repeat)
    • Inside to outside (alternate feet): Footwork for trapping: L-DOR-L-TR-L-DR-LR-DOL-R-TL-R-DL-RL. Footwork for no trapping: (L-)DOR-L-DR-LR-DOL-R-DL-RL (repeat).
    • Three-Touch per foot: Do three touches with one foot, inside to outside to inside. Last inside touch transfers the ball to the other foot. Footwork: (L)-DIR-L-DOR-L-DIR-LR-DIL-R-DOL-R-DIL-RL
  • Rollover Cuts
    • Trap Dance: Start with one foot on top of ball. Roll ball over to other foot, putting other foot on top of ball. Repeat.
    • Rollover to inside trap: RolR-LR-TL-R-RolL-RL-TR-L (repeat)
    • Outside to rollover (same foot): Footwork (right foot): L-DOR-L-RR (repeat)
    • Triangles: Start with ball in front. Use one foot to roll ball back. Catch the ball with the same foot and move ball to other foot. Kick back to original position and repeat. Switch the foot that rolls the ball back. Also try kicking behind your foot.
  • Juggling: Kick ball repeatedly, keeping ball in the air.

Grain Dish
    4 cups vegetable stock
    1/2 cup barley
    1/3 cup wild rice
    1/4 cup brown rice
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 onion diced
    1/2 pound sliced mushrooms
    1 14.5-oz can finely chopped canned tomatoes, with their juice
    1 teaspoon grated lemon peel
    1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley (or 4 t dried parsley)
    2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh thyme (or $\frac12$ t dried thyme)
    1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh basil (or $\frac12$ t dried basil)
    1/2 cup bulghur
    2 cups cubed sharp cheddar cheese
  • Bring the vegetable stock to a boil in a medium-size saucepan. Add the barley and wild rice and simmer, covered, for 15 minutes. Stir in the brown rice and cook 10 minutes more. Pour the entire contents into a baking dish.
  • Heat the oil in a medium size skillet. Add the onion and saute until soft. Stir in the mushrooms and cook until they begin to get brown and juicy, about 10 minutes. Scrape the mixture into the grains.
  • Stir in the tomatoes, lemon peel, parsley, thyme, and basil. Cover the dish and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  • Remove the casserole from the oven and stir in the bulghur. Cover again and return to the oven. Cook 25 more minutes.
  • Fluff the mixture with a fork and gently stir in the cheese. Cover again and let sit outside the oven for 10 minutes before serving.

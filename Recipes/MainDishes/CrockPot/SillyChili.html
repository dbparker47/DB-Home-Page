Silly Chili
    1/2-1 pound ground round
    1 quart diced tomatoes
    1 chopped onion
    3 cloves garlic, minced
    1 pound corn
    2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
    1 tablespoon chili powder (original called for 2 tablespoons)
    1 teaspoon ground cumin
    1 teaspoon dried marjoram or oregano
    1-2 tablespoons honey
    1 bay leaf
    16 ounces tomato sauce
    30 ounces spicy chili beans
    15 ounces dark red kidney beans
    15 ounces garbanzo beans
    1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
    Cheddar cheese, oyster crackers, plain yogurt, and hot pepper sauce to taste
  • Drain and rinse kidney and garbanzo beans.
  • Place and stir together all ingredients in crockpot (except the cheese, crackers, yogurt and hot sauce, you silly!).
  • Cook on low for 10--12 hours.

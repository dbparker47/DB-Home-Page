Molten Chocolate Cake (Mug)
    4 tablespoons butter
    1 ounce bittersweet chocolate, chopped
    1 ounce bittersweet chocolate, broken into 4 equal pieces
    1/4 cup sugar
    2 eggs
    2 tablespoons cocoa powder
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1/4 teaspoon salt
    1/4 cup flour
    1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    All microwaving is at 50% power
  • Microwave butter and chopped chocolate in large bowl, stirring often, until melted, about 1 minute. Whisk sugar, eggs, cocoa, vanilla, and salt into chocolate mixture until smooth. In separate bowl, combine flour and baking powder. Whisk flour mixture into chocolate mixture until combined. Divide batter evenly between 2 (11-ounce) coffee mugs.
  • Place mugs on opposite sides of microwave turntable. Microwave at 50 percent power for 50 seconds (35 seconds for single mug). Stir batter and microwave at 50 percent power for 50 seconds (35 seconds for single mug) (batter will rise to just below rim of mug). Press 2 chocolate pieces into center of each cake until chocolate is flush with top of cake. Microwave at 50 percent power for 40 seconds (20 seconds for single mug) (cake should be slightly wet around edges of mug and somewhat drier toward center). Let cakes rest for 2 minutes. Serve.

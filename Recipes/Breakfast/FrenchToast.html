French Toast
    2/3 cup milk
    4 extra-large eggs
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
    Lots of bread
  • Whisk together all non-bread ingredients in a shallow bowl.
  • Turn the bread slices in the egg mixture until thoroughly saturated but not falling apart.
  • Cook on the griddle.

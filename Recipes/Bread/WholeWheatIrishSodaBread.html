Whole Wheat Irish Soda Bread
    2 1/2 cups white unbleached flour
    2 cups whole wheat flour
    1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
    2 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
    1 teaspoon salt (optional)
    2 tablespoons butter
    2 eggs
    1 1/2 cups buttermilk
    2 tablespoons carroway seeds
    1 cups golden raisins
  • Combine white flour, wheat flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  • Work in butter with pastry blender (or food processor?)
  • In a separate bowl, thoroughly mix eggs with buttermilk
  • Add carroway seeds and raisins.
  • Mix the whole mess, and knead until smooth.
  • Form into a flat 8-inch circle on a greased cookie sheet.
  • Cut an ``X'' into the top with a flour-covered knife.
  • Bake at 350 degrees for 45-55 minutes in the center of the oven. Tap on bottom to see if it is done.
  • Lightly butter on top of loaf, and cool on rack.

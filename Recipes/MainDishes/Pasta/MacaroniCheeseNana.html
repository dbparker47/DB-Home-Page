Nana's Macaroni & Cheese
    4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni noodles (4 pounds?)
    1 onion, chopped
    4 tablespoons butter
    4 tablespoons flour
    1 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1/2 teaspoon pepper (optional)
    4 cups milk
    4 cups cheese (shredded)
    1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
    Bread crumbs on top
  • Combine and bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes.

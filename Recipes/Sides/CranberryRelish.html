Cranberry Relish (Mama Stamberg's)
    4 cups whole raw cranberries, washed (16 ounces)
    1 medium onion (small?)
    1 1/2 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
    1 cup sugar
    4 tablespoons horseradish from a jar
  • Grind the raw berries and onion together. (I use an old-fashioned meat grinder. I'm sure there's a setting on the food processor that will give you a chunky grind â€” not a puree.)
  • Add everything else and mix. Put in a plastic container and freeze.
  • Early morning, move it from freezer to refrigerator compartment to thaw. (It should still have some little icy slivers left.)

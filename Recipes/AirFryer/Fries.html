Fries/Squash
    Potatoes, sweet potatoes, or peeled squash, washed and sliced into fries or cubes
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    Seasonings (e.g. salt, pepper, smoked paprika, )
  • Slice potatoes/squash into fries or cubes.
  • In a mixing bowl, mix oil and seasonings. Add the potatoes/squash and toss through the oil/seasoning mixture.
  • Place the fries/cubes in the air fryer basket. Air fry at 400 degrees for 13 minutes (fries) or 19 minutes? (squash), tossing halfway through.

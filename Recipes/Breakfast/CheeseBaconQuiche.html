Cheese and Bacon Quiche
    Pie Crust (1/2 recipe)
    1/4 cup butter (softened)
    4 eggs
    2 cups heavy cream
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/2 cup grated Swiss cheese
    1 tablespoon butter
    3/4 cup chopped cooked bacon (12 ounces, uncooked)
    2 tablespoons minced green onions
    pinch of pepper
    dab of stinky cheese (Stilton or Camembert will work)
  • Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  • Spread 1/4 cup of butter onto the pie crust evenly, then chill.
  • In a large bowl, beat eggs, heavy cream, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt with a whisk. Stir in the grated cheese.
  • Melt the tablespoon of butter in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Add the chopped bacon, minced green onions, 1/4 teaspoon of salt and pinch of pepper. Cook for 5 minutes or until the onions are tender, stirring frequently.
  • Stir the bacon and onion mixture into the cream mixture
  • Pour into pie and bake for 15 minutes at 425 degrees.
  • Reduce heat to 325 degrees and bake for another 35 minutes.
  • Allow to stand for 10 to 15 minutes.
  • Spoon a dab of soft stinky chess on top before serving piping hot.

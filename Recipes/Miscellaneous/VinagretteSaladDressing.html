Vinagrette Dressing
    2 cloves garlic, minced
    1 tablespoon dijon mustard
    1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
    1/4 teaspoon sugar
    1/4 teaspoon pepper (optional)
    1/3 cup vinegar (balsamic, apple cider, rice, others?)
    5/12 cup oil (olive or canola), so that vinegar and oil combine for 3/4 cup
    Used in: Santa Rosa Chicken Salad

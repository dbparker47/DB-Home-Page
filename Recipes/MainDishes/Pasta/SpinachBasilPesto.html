Spinach Basil Pesto
    1 cup olive oil
    1 jar sun-dried tomatoes (Trader Joe's)
    3-4 cloves garlic
    1 wedge feta cheese (Trader Joe's)
    Some chopped fresh basil (1 package)
    10 oz spinach, chopped
    1-2 red peppers, chopped
    1/2 to 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
    1/3 cup pine nuts, lightly toasted
    Fusilli pasta
  • Mix ingredients (except pine nuts) in a bowl. Let sit all day. Stir occasionally.

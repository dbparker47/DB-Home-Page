Sourdough Starter
  • Day 1: In the morning, combine a little less than 1/2 cup flour and an appropriate amount of water. In the evening, stir vigorously.
  • Days 2, 3, & 4: Add a little less than 1/4 cup flour each morning with the appropriate amount of water. In the evening, stir vigorously.
  • Days 5 & 6: Add a little less than 1/2 cup flour each morning with the appropriate amount of water. In the evening, stir vigorously. Yeast should be very active by now. This leaves you with a little more than 1 cup starter.
  • To put starter to sleep, let it sit 2 hours after a feeding at room temperature, add flour to dry it out into a ball, and refrigerate.
  • To wake up starter (a few days before baking bread), bring to room temperature, feed with same amount of water and flour as above twice daily (discarding 80% each time), until starter is lively.
    Used in: Whole Wheat Sourdough Bread and Sourdough Pancakes & Waffles

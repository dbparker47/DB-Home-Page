Pasta Fresca
    4 c chopped ripe tomatoes
    6-8 large basil leaves
    1 large garlic clove
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    salt and pepper to taste
    1 pound bow-tie pasta
    1/2 pound mozzarella cheese, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
    grated parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Bring a large pot of water to a rapid boil.
  • In a blender or food processor, puree the tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil until smooth. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • Cook the pasta. Drain. Toss the pasta immediately with the cubed mozzarella cheese, Add the sauce and mix well. Top with grated parmesan cheese.
